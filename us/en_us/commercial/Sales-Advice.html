Expert Sales Advice

Sales Questions? Let’s Talk.

Whether you’re wondering how Vitamix can work for your business or trying to determine which Vitamix commercial machine best fits your needs, our dedicated sales representatives will answer all of your product questions. Simply fill out the form below and tell us about yourself. We’ll follow up with you within two business days to answer your questions and help you reach a decision. There’s no pressure—we’re just here to help. At the end of our call, you’ll know which products we recommend for you and where to find your nearest Vitamix dealer.

We Can Help

  • Discover if Vitamix Is The Right Partner For You
    Because we share your passion for great food and great service, we’ll spend some time getting to know your business—the dishes you prepare in a blender, your average batch sizes, and how turnkey your blending needs to be.
  • Decide Which Machine Best Fits Your Needs
    Based on our conversation, we’ll narrow the product offering down to one or two high-performance blenders we recommend for your kitchen.
  • Determine Where To Purchase Your Vitamix Equipment
    When you’re ready to take the next step, you’ll have all the information you need to order online or walk into your nearest authorized dealer.
