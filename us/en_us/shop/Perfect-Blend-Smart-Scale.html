Features

The Perfect Blend™ smart scale and interactive recipe app make your life in the kitchen easier by walking you step-by-step through recipes, helping you meal plan and stick to nutritional goals, preparing shopping lists, and more.

  • Personalize your experience: Choose from a growing collection of recipes or add your own to the app. You can also search recipes by dietary preference or what’s in your pantry.
  • Perfect portions: Automatically scale recipes to desired number of servings, ounces, or calories.
  • Monitor nutritional information: You’ll see nutritional information for every ingredient, as you add it to the container.
  • Convenient planning: Select a pre-designed meal plan to follow and create customized shopping lists.
  • Built-In Timer: Track blending progress to achieve your perfect consistency, every time.
  • Track your health: Connect the scale to your favorite fitness apps through Apple Health & Google Fit.
  • Guaranteed: 1 year manufacturer's warranty on the PerfectBlend smart scale
