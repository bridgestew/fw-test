Features

Designed to process medium batches with a lightweight design that fits easily under most kitchen cabinets.

Tidy Blends

Every angle of the Vitamix container is designed to create our uniquely controlled vortex, systematically folding ingredients back to the blades for faster, smoother blends.

Letting Off Steam

The vented lid and lid plug let you bring dishes to hot serving temperatures right in the container, or add ingredients while blending to adjust texture.

Medium Batches

The size and shape of the 48-ounce container is ideal for blending medium batches for small family meals.

Leak Prevention

Our containers are designed to prevent leaks from entering the motor base, which is the number one cause of failure in lower-quality blenders.
