Features

A 48-ounce container processes medium batches of your favorite recipes, while fitting easily under most kitchen cabinets.

Medium Batches

The size and shape of the 48-ounce container is ideal for blending medium batches for small family meals.

You’re In Control

The Variable Speed Control lets you fine-tune every texture—from smooth purées to chunky salsas—and everything in between.

Mini-Tamper

Customized for smaller containers, the Mini-Tamper lets you blend thick, frozen treats and dense nut butters—without endless attachments that would need to be cleaned and stored.

Easy Cleaning

With a drop of dish soap and warm water, your Vitamix machine can clean itself in 30 to 60 seconds.

Food Prep

Batters

Batters

Smoothies & Juices

Smoothies & Juices

Turn healthy fruits and vegetables into refreshing smoothies and juices. The Vitamix blender uses the whole ingredient, including the fiber, for a great snack or complement to any healthy breakfast.

Nut Butters

Nut Butters

Making your own natural, preservative-free nut butters is so easy. Try a variety of flavors for sandwiches, fruit dips, healthy snacks, or a peanut butter smoothie. Almond butter can also add nutty flavor to Asian soups and sauces.

Non-Dairy Milks

Non-Dairy Milks

Create inexpensive, non-dairy milk for many lactose-free recipes, whether pouring over cereal or making smoothie recipes, overnight oats, or vegan mac and cheese.

Hot Soups

Hot Soups

The friction of our blades can make steaming-hot soup in under 10 minutes, right in the container; chilled soups take even less time. Make each blend your own by playing with add-ins like ramen noodles, tortellini, or kefir.

Frozen Desserts

Frozen Desserts

Your Vitamix blender will quickly and easily create chilled desserts for you, from ice cream and sherbet to fruit sorbet and milkshakes.If you've always wanted to know how to make ice cream, look no further.

Flours & Doughs

Flours & Doughs

Dressings & Sauces

Dressings & Sauces

Dips & Spreads

Dips & Spreads
