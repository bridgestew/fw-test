Mango Smoothie

Mango, orange and vanilla yogurt make this smoothie sweet and creamy with a light citrus note.

Submitted By: Vitamix

Dietary Interests: Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, Low Cholesterol, Low Fat, Low Sodium

Yield:

3 cups (720 ml)

Total Time:

11 Minutes

Print:

8.5x11

Difficulty:

Easy

Save To:

Recipe Book

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup (180 ml) low fat vanilla yogurt
  • ¼ cup (60 ml) water
  • 1 orange, peeled, halved
  • 1 cup (150 g) frozen mango
  • ½ cup (70 g) ice cubes

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients into the Vitamix container in the order listed and secure lid.
  2. Select Variable 1.
  3. Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to Variable 10, then to High.
  4. Blend for 30 seconds to 1 minute or until desired consistency is reached. Serve immediately.
