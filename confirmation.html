Tips and Tricks

Your product is coming soon, so here are some helpful tips to get you started as soon as we hit your doorstep.

Using the Tamper

The tamper lets you blend thick and frozen mixtures like hummus and ice cream—without stopping the machine. With the lid on and the machine running, just remove the lid plug and press ingredients down with the tamper. Don’t worry—it won’t touch the blades.

Cleaning Your Machine

Every Vitamix machine is super easy to clean. Our S-Series containers and blades are dishwasher-safe, so just place them on the top rack. The C- and G-Series containers clean themselves with some warm water, a drop of dish soap and 60 seconds on your blender’s highest speed. Voila, you’re ready for another blend.
